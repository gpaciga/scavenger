Scavenger Hunt

Instructions

Have everybody on a video call and load this webpage. As a group, decide on a magic word and a time limit. Have everybody enter the same magic word into the field below. Then, everybody click the "GO!" button at the same time. Everybody will be given the same random list of items. You should then all run around trying to get as many things on the list as possible. Whoever manages to find the most items within the time limit wins!
